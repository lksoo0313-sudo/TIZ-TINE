# 📌 TIZ-TINE Official Design & Prompt Guide (v1.1)

This document defines the visual identity and prompt engineering rules for the character **TIZ-TINE**. All AI-generated assets must follow these guidelines to maintain character consistency.

---

## 1. Core Character Identity (Base Prompt)

**Character Concept:** A cute humanoid Maltese girl with a "Hip & Emotional" street aesthetic, deeply connected to music.

### 1.1 Physical Traits
- **Hair:** Short, fluffy white bob cut with soft natural volume.
- **Ears:** Small floppy dog ears, light brown color.
- **Eyes:** Large, round, glossy eyes (dark brown/black).
- **Face:** Tiny black nose, smooth fair skin, soft youthful features, subtle cheek blush.
- **Build:** Small, slightly slender build with human-like proportions.

### 1.2 Signature Accessories (MANDATORY)
- **Audio Gear:** Always wearing stylish over-ear headphones (on ears or around neck) or trendy wireless earphones. This reflects her identity as a music-centric character.

### 1.3 Personality & Expression
- **Expression:** Gentle, slightly playful, innocent, yet cool and confident.
- **Voice (Silent):** Communicates through mood and style rather than words.

### 1.4 Rendering Specs
- **Style:** Ultra-clean 3D anime style, high-end character rendering.
- **Lighting:** Soft cinematic lighting, smooth shading, detailed textures.
- **Resolution:** 8K, sharp focus.

---

## 2. Consistency Rules
- **Structure:** Maintain the same face structure, eye size, and body proportions in every scene.
- **Assets:** Never change the hair style (white bob) or ear shape/color (light brown floppy).
- **Aesthetic:** Always preserve the "Cute + Soft + High-quality 3D" look.
- **Identity:** Never omit the headphones/earphones; they are her core signature.
